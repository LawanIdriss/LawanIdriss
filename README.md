## About Seamium

Seamium builds autonomous energy systems for modern compute infrastructure. The company develops an engine that governs how AI workloads and supporting systems consume energy, based on real availability, cost, and operating limits.

Rather than treating energy as a fixed input or downstream concern, Seamium makes it a first-class control signal within infrastructure operations.



## What the engine does

- Observes energy and system conditions in real time  
- Governs workload behavior in response to availability, cost, and constraints  
- Adapts continuously instead of relying on static rules  
- Detects faults early and maintains stability over time  
- Operates securely with minimal human intervention  


## Why it matters

Energy is no longer an abstract utility. It shapes cost, reliability, and the ability to scale compute systems. Seamium replaces manual coordination and rigid controls with autonomous behavior, allowing infrastructure to remain efficient, accountable, and resilient as conditions change.
